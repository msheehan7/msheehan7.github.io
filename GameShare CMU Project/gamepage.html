Minecraft

Minecraft has gained intense popularity because of how it allows the player to build whatever they can think of. This allows the user to be much happier with the game and the outcome of what they do. Minecraft is friendly to all users and the experience is non-comparable. Minecraft got reviews of 93 on Metacritic, 91% TechSpot, and a 9/10 on IGN.com.

  • Developed by Markus Persson and Mojang, published by Mojang, Xbox Game Studios, Sony Computer Entertainment.
  • Available for Microsoft Windows, MacOs, Linux, and all consoles.
  • Single player, online multiplayer, and local multiplayer
