GameShare

GameShare was my final project at the CMU SAMS program. In an effort to create a
working website in only 1.5 weeks 3 other people and me embarked on a
journey to create a working game trading webstie. This website
used Javascript, HTML, CSS, and Firebase to create a working
product, that allows one to trade games with others trough our company.
Trading first involves a working user database, requiring an initial sign-up, which allows you to save games to
your profile, attempt to trade your game for other's, and await
confirmation from those you wish to trade with.

Sams-Tagram 2.0

In the Sams-Tagram 2.0 News Feed you can post pictures and text to
a board. There is also an X in the corner of every post so that you can
remove the post whenever you want. A link to the profile page of Andrew
Carnegie can be found in the right hand corner of the website, and
when clicked you can view and change the username, bio, and image
of Andrew Carnegie.

Todo List

This reminder board allows you to post your daily "todos" as if they
were post-it notes. When they are posted to the page they will be
removed when you click on them.

Typing Game

In this typing game the objective is to type the words that appear on the page
as fast as possible. When you type these words in they will
dissapear, but be prepared becasue there is no stopping them.
